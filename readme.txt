= Is it compatible with other plugins? =

We have tested Cost Of Goods with many third-party plugins and themes.

= How do I set up product costs? =

Navigate to any product page within WooCommerce, and you will find a new field to enter the cost of goods sold directly below the regular pricing fields.

= Can I track costs over time? =

Absolutely! Our plugin provides historical data tracking, allowing you to view changes in costs and their impact on your profitability over any given period.

= Is premium support available? =

Premium users have access to priority support. All users can access our online documentation and community forums for assistance.

= Can I use this plugin to calculate my profit margins? =

Yes, by entering the cost of goods for each product, the plugin will help you calculate your profit margins, giving you a clear understanding of your financial health.

= Does the plugin support variable products? =

Yes, the premium version allows you to set Cost Of Goods prices for variation products, providing comprehensive tracking and management of all your product types.

= How does this plugin help in managing COGS? =

The plugin provides an easy interface to input and update your cost of goods, track changes over time, and analyze the impact on your profitability, ensuring you have accurate data to make informed business decisions.
